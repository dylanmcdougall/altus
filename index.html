Features

Dark Theme

Altus comes with an inbuilt dark theme which you can toggle on/off at your convenience. The dark theme provided is a courtesy of vednoc.

Custom Themes

If you are skilled at CSS, you can also write your own themes or use themes provided by other people.

Notifications

Get native notifications on all the platforms including Windows (7 or higher), Linux and MacOS.

Tray Support

Tray icon support so you can minimize the app completely and still get notified.
